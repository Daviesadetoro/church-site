Frequently Asked Questions

What must i do to be saved

+

Hear the gospel-Rom 10:17, Believe the Gospel - Rom 1:16, Repent of your Sins - Luke 13:3,5, Confess christ as the son of God - Rom 10:10, Be Baptized - Mark 16:15,16.

Why should i be baptized

+

Through baptism a person comes "into Christ"."For as many of you as were baptized int Christ have put on Christ":(Gal 3:27), At Baptism a person recieves forgiveness of sins. "And now why are you waiting? Arise and be baptized and wash away your sins, calling on the name of the Lord" (Acts 22:16).
